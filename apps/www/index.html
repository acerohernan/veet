How does it work?

We re-engineered the service we built for secure bussiness meetings, to make it free and avaliable for all.

Free for live demo call to test the quality of application without registering or leaving any personal data.
Once you've enter the demo call, you'll buttons on the bottom to turn on/off your microphone and camera. The quality of the video will be regulated according to your network connection.
You will be able to create links to invite any person for jonining the demo room for free.
